<picture>:
- it's similar to srcset and sizes attributes in <img> but this one is more flexible and clearer.
- it's purpose is to optimize page to minimize bandwidth usage and to make the loading time faster.
- can be used as art direction to display different photos in different devices
- the img is compulsory: <img class="example" src="img/small.jpg" alt="masjid al-aqsa" /> - if the browser does not support picture, then the img will be used.
- this will be easier to read when compared to by adding the srcset and sizes in img tag
